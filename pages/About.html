About Our Company

Our company was founded by Roberto Hernandez and Karen Jimenez who loved coffee so much that they decided to make a coffee shop that made every different type of coffee out there in the world and provide it to those coffee lovers out there just like themselves.

Their dream originated from a small town in Hialeah, Florida.
